Random song

To listen to a random song, click here, or use the normal url of the webpage wihtout any parameters.

Specific song

To listen to a specific song from a model, use the url of the webpage with parameters /song/{model_name}/{instrument}.

To generate a song

To generate a song from a model, use the url of the webpage with parameters /generate/{model_name}/{instrument}.
Number of songs is an optional last parameter: /generate/{model_name}/{instrument}/{count} will generate {count} songs.
